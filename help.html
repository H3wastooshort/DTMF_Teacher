DTMF Teacher Help

This tool is meant to teach you how to tell DTMF tones by ear. In the end, you might be able to decypher phone numbers only by ear, while they are getting typed into a phone.

This is a dialpad, including the special A, B, C, and D keys:

1 2 3 A
4 5 6 B
7 8 9 C
* 0 # D

single row or column mode
